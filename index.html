Leather Restoration, Repairs & Reconditioning

LEATHER RESTORATION Luxury Leather and Furniture Care (LLFC) is a leather care company who can almost restore almost all leather articles. How does our restoration work? The first step is to clean thoroughly to remove all the dirt and the move towards next step that is to dye with original colour so the leather can get original look. REPAIR / REPLACE Repair of leather is related to, repair minor torn, scratches, which can done with our experienced technicians. Sometimes there could need of restoration due to minor work. Repair also could be as stitching. Replacement of particular panel if that cannot be repaired. If we have to replace something there will be customers consent and the finish will be intact Luxury Leather and Furniture Care Company is established with high trained technicians and well management too, providing services across India with Pick and Drop facilities.
